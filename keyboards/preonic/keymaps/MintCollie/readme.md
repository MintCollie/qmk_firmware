# Preonic layout by [MintCollie](https://github.com/MintCollie)

## Layouts

### MINT
a edit of the default Qwerty keymap with some basic changes that are more prefreable for me.
```
,-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------.
|  Esc |   1  |   2  |   3  |   4  |   5  |   6  |   7  |   8  |   9  |   0  | Bksp |
|------+------+------+------+------+------+------+------+------+------+------+------|
| Tab  |   Q  |   W  |   E  |   R  |   T  |   Y  |   U  |   I  |   O  |   P  | Del  |
|------+------+------+------+------+-------------+------+------+------+------+------|
|  `   |   A  |   S  |   D  |   F  |   G  |   H  |   J  |   K  |   L  |   ;  |  "   |
|------+------+------+------+------+------|------+------+------+------+------+------|
| Shift|   Z  |   X  |   C  |   V  |   B  |   N  |   M  |   ,  |   .  |   /  | Enter|
|------+------+------+------+------+------+------+------+------+------+------+------|
| Ctrl | GUI  | Alt  | Brite|Lower |    Space    |Raise | Left | Down | Up   | Right|
`-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------'
```
